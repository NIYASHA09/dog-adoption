POPULAR BREEDS

  • Labrador Retriever

    The Labrador Retriever is one of the most popular breeds in the United States. They are friendly, outgoing, and great with children and other animals.

  • German Shepherd

    The German Shepherd is a versatile breed that can be used for police work, search and rescue, and even as a family pet. They are loyal, intelligent, and protective.

  • Poodle

    The Poodle is a highly intelligent and trainable breed. They come in three different sizes (standard, miniature, and toy) and are known for their curly coats.
